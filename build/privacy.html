Your privacy matters to us. Here's how we protect and handle your data.

Effective Date: January 1, 2025

1. Information We Collect

Personal Information:

  • Email address and authentication credentials
  • Profile information you provide
  • Subscription and payment information (processed by RevenueCat)

Usage Data:

  • Habit tracking data (habits, routines, completion dates)
  • Mood logging entries (mood ratings, tags, journal entries)
  • App usage analytics and performance data
  • Device information and app preferences (themes, notification settings)

Automatically Collected:

  • Device identifiers and operating system information
  • App crash reports and error logs (via Firebase Crashlytics)
  • App performance and stability data
  • Push notification tokens

2. How We Use Your Information

  • Provide and maintain the FruitFul app service
  • Sync your data across devices via Firebase
  • Send smart notifications for habits and mood reminders
  • Process subscription payments through RevenueCat
  • Improve app performance and user experience
  • Provide customer support

3. Data Storage and Security

  • Data is stored securely using Google Firebase services
  • All data transmission is encrypted using industry-standard protocols
  • We implement appropriate security measures to protect your personal information
  • Crash reports are processed securely by Firebase Crashlytics for app stability
  • Subscription data is processed securely by RevenueCat

4. Data Sharing

We DO NOT sell your personal data. We may share data with:

  • Firebase/Google: For data storage, authentication, and crash reporting
  • Firebase Crashlytics: For app crash reports and performance monitoring
  • RevenueCat: For subscription management and payment processing
  • Apple: For app store functionality and push notifications
  • Legal Requirements: When required by law or to protect our rights

5. Your Rights

  • Access, update, or delete your personal data
  • Opt-out of non-essential notifications
  • Cancel subscriptions through your app store account
  • Request data deletion upon account termination
  • Contact us for data access requests

6. Data Retention

  • Account data: Retained while your account is active
  • Habit and mood data: Retained until you delete it or close your account
  • Analytics data: Retained for up to 2 years
  • Deleted accounts: Data permanently removed within 30 days

7. Children's Privacy

FruitFul is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

8. International Data Transfers

Your data may be transferred to and processed in countries other than your own, including the United States, where our service providers operate.

9. Changes to Privacy Policy

We may update this policy periodically. We'll notify you of significant changes through the app or email.

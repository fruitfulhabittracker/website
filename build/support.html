You can cancel your FruitFul subscription anytime through your App Store account. Go to Settings > Apple ID > Subscriptions, find FruitFul, and tap Cancel Subscription. Your access will continue until the end of your current billing period.

Your habits and mood data automatically sync across all your devices when you're signed in with the same account. Make sure you're connected to the internet and logged in to see your data sync seamlessly.

FruitFul comes with 5 beautiful pre-designed themes: Light, Dark, Fruitful, Zen, and That Girl. You can switch between themes in your app settings to find the perfect look that matches your style and preferences.

FruitFul's smart notifications only remind you about habits you haven't completed and mood logging when you haven't checked in. You won't get spam notifications - just helpful nudges when you need them most.

Your data is safely stored in the cloud, so deleting the app won't delete your habits and progress. When you reinstall and sign back in, all your data will be restored. To permanently delete your data, use the "Delete Account" option in Settings.

With FruitFul Premium, you can track unlimited habits and organize them into custom routines. There's no limit to how many positive changes you can make in your life!
